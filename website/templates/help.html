Game Tracking Website Help

Welcome to our game tracking website!

We are excited to help you keep track of your favorite games and stay up-to-date on the latest news and updates. Here are some tips to help you get started:

How to create an account:

To create an account, click on the "Sign up" button in the top right corner of the screen. Fill in your email address and choose a password. You will receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

How to add a game to your tracking list:

Once you're logged in, go to the "Games" page and search for the game you want to track. Click on the "Track" button to add it to your tracking list. You will receive notifications when there are updates or news about your tracked games.

How to customize your profile:

To customize your profile, go to the "Profile" page and click on the "Edit Profile" button. You can upload a profile picture, add a bio, and customize your notification settings.

How to use the search feature:

To search for a game or news article, use the search bar located in the top right corner of the screen. You can search for games by title or platform, and news articles by keyword or topic.

How to contact support:

If you have any questions or issues, you can contact our support team by clicking on the "Contact" button in the footer of the website. Fill out the form with your name, email, and message, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.

Thank you for using our game tracking website!
